Our Profile

Indian Business & Professional Council (IBPCRAK) established in 2012 is a non-profit body of Indian businessmen and professionals based in Ras Al Khaimah in particular and U.A.E in general. It represents Indian Business & Professional community in the emirate before the Government and non Governmental authorities to address individual problem or problems of a trade or to solve general problems of trade and industries of India & U.A.E. It also enjoys support and recognition of both the Indian embassy in Abu Dhabi and Consulate General of India, Dubai. IBPCRAK facilitates Indian Business & Professional community, in whatever ways possible to augment trade and commerce between the nations.
